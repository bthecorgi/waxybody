Hi there!

My name is Keith and this is my 5th year offering Ski and Snowboard Services in the Mississauga area. I do all my services by hand, which elongates the life of your equipment.

Here are my prices for skis and snowboards:

  • $25 - Hot Wax and Sharpen
  • $15 - Hot wax only (including scrape and texture brush)
  • $5 - Storage wax scrape and texture brush (if you sealed your equipment with storage wax last season)
  • $10 - Minor Base repair

Cross Country Ski Services:

  • $15 - Hot wax only, scrape and texture brush
  • $5 - Glide wax (Waxless skis)

Don't forget to let me know what conditions/temperature you will be skiing in since I carry a variety of waxes.

I always look forward to swapping stories about ski and snowboard experiences. Love chatting with both novice and experienced riders because I love learning from others and sharing my knowledge with others.

I try to work around everyone's schedules with pick-up and drop-offs before or after work. The best way to contact me is through the kijiji app messenger.

Happy to answer any questions you have!
